Portfolio of my Art by Jasmine Godoy

---A drawing I made for Valentime's Day that I had published on my schools newsletter.I made this through krita while drawing on my wacom tablet only took me a couple of hours. I make stickers, logos, designs, and work on freelance work.---

I made this for a coloring book that I was working on. During the time I was at Tulsatech I worked on several projects including a cover,illustration pages, and sketeches of our final rough draft of designs. My coloring/sticker book was made specifically for kids so they could color and learn about recipes around the world. Each page featuring a new reciepe from each country.

This is a progress sheet that I was working on to make stickers usually when I make stickers my process is start with a sketch, have a folder or album of referenece pictures and paste your palette plus the photos on a layer. Then afterwards freehand the drawing in your own style and draw a thin white outline around it and VIOLA you got a wonderful design you can sell.

When I doodle I get inspiration from pinterest and when it comes to the color palettes I use I tend to stick to pastel coloring but sometimes if the drawing calls for monotone,complementary,monochromatic, or analogous color schemes I use those instead.

They say eyes are the window to the soul and in this drawing I did exactly that. Once I get the inspiration to draw something I spend hours on it like in this drawing. I start with my base which is the sketch then move onto the outline and then ontop of that is coloring for each piece. And adding the finishing touches.
